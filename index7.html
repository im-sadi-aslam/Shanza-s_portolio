International Teaching Experience

I have successfully taught students from diverse international backgrounds, including those from the United Kingdom, Canada, the United States, and Australia. I am experienced in delivering tailored online lessons based on a variety of international curricula such as:

  • British Curriculum (KS2–KS4, IGCSE, O Levels)
  • Canadian Curriculum
  • American Curriculum (Common Core, High School Math & English)
  • Australian Curriculum (including VCE – Further Mathematics and English)

My teaching approach emphasizes conceptual clarity, exam preparation, and skill-based learning. I use interactive tools, worksheets, and customized lesson plans to meet individual learning needs and cultural contexts.

Test Preparation Experience

As an experienced and dedicated educator, I have successfully prepared students for a variety of standardized and university admission tests, including:

  • STS (Sindh Testing Service)
  • IBA Aptitude Test
  • GRE (Graduate Record Examination)
  • NAT (National Aptitude Test)
  • GAT (Graduate Assessment Test)
  • AKU Admission Test (Aga Khan University)

My test prep sessions focus on concept-building, strategic problem-solving, and effective time management, tailored to individual student needs.
